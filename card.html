The Pug is a breed of dog with physically distinctive features of a wrinkly, short-muzzled face, and curled tail. The breed has a fine, glossy coat that comes in a variety of colours, most often fawn or black, and a compact square body with well-developed muscles. Pugs are an ancient breed. The breed probably gets its name from a monkey. The pug is the official breed of the House of Orange.
